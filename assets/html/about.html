About


So you came back to learn more about Marc F. Munic.


He got those sunglasses for free at the Archer panel at Comic-con. That shirt he got for free at the Anitiques Road Show (if you unaware of what that is, ask the oldest person you know), because the guy at the merch table thought he was Zach Galifianakis(...the guy from the hangover).


At the time of writing this website, Marc is 33 years old. That is the same age as Jesus when he was crucified. This is the bar Marc checks for how well he is doing in life. So far he has not been sentenced to capital punishment for treason. This sounds like a good thing, but if he ever is, his last thought will probably be, "I'm behind the curve."


When designing websites, Marc likes a strong header. He doesn't prefer jumbotrons, but gets why other people do. When possible he uses percentages over pixels. Marc uses tabs, but doesn't have a strong opinion about spaces.


Finally, Marc doesn't normally talk in the third person, and he finds writing bios of himself to be horribly bizarre.
