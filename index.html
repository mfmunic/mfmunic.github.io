About Me

Marc F. Munic is a Navy Veteran and is very active is multiple community groups. He is an elected Board Member and Treasurer for the City Heights Town Council, Inc., and Vice Chair for the City Heights Area Planning Committee. His active involvement in and commitment to the improvement of his neighborhood and community, since moving to San Diego in 2004, has led to him to running a number of local political campaigns. This unique experience in creating public relations strategies, media campaigns, creating an extensive government relations network, budget analysis, fundraising, and utilizing market research has made Marc versatile and flexible enough to handle any opportunity.
